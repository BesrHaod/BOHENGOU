Who We Are

Rooted in Legacy,
Built for Tomorrow

Zhijiang Bohengou Trading Co., Ltd. stands at the intersection of time-honored commercial wisdom and contemporary global trade.

Headquartered in the historic city of Zhijiang, Yichang — a region renowned for its rich cultural heritage and strategic economic significance — we are a modern trading enterprise committed to delivering excellence across every transaction, partnership, and endeavor.

Our name, Bohengou (柏很古), evokes the enduring strength of the cypress tree and the timeless values of integrity, resilience, and prosperity. These principles guide every facet of our operations.
