Privacy Policy

App: StillGrid – Collage Maker
Package name: com.stillgrid.collage
Effective date: June 21, 2026
Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how StillGrid (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information when you use our Android application StillGrid – Collage Maker (the “App”).

StillGrid is an offline collage editor. We do not operate user accounts, and we do not sell personal information. This policy is written to match the App’s actual behavior and declared permissions.

1. Summary

  • Photo editing happens on your device.
  • We do not collect personal data on our own servers because the App does not send your data to us.
  • The App does not declare Internet, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage permissions.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or crash-reporting services that transmit your data to us.
  • Some app settings and project data are stored locally on your device and may be included in Android backup if you enable it.

2. Information the App Processes

The App processes information only to provide collage editing features. We do not require you to create an account or provide your name, email address, or phone number to use the App.

2.1 Photos and media you choose

When you pick photos, replace a cell image, or open images shared from another app, the App accesses only the items you select through Android system pickers or share intents. Images are loaded and edited on your device to display previews and to create exported collages.

We do not upload your photos to our servers. Photos remain under your control on your device unless you export them to your gallery or share them through Android’s share sheet.

2.2 Project and editing data stored locally

To support drafts and recent projects, the App stores data locally on your device, including:

  • Project titles and editing state (layout, spacing, text, backgrounds, and related settings)
  • References to photos you selected (such as content URIs), not copies of your entire photo library
  • Optional thumbnail cache files to speed up recent-project previews

This data is stored in the App’s local database and app storage. Uninstalling the App removes this local app data from your device, except for images you already exported to your gallery.

2.3 App preferences

The App stores local preferences such as:

  • Whether onboarding was completed
  • Theme choice (system, light, or dark)
  • Default export format, quality, and resolution

2.4 Exported images

When you export a collage, the App saves an image file to your device gallery in a Pictures/StillGrid folder using Android’s MediaStore APIs. Export happens locally on your device. We do not receive exported files.

3. Android Permissions

Based on the App’s manifest, StillGrid does not request dangerous runtime permissions such as Internet access, camera access, location access, microphone access, or broad storage access.

The App uses standard Android platform features instead, including:

  • Photo Picker / visual media picker – for you to choose photos without granting the App full gallery access on supported Android versions
  • MediaStore – to save exported images you choose to create
  • Share intents – to open images shared to the App from other apps, only when you initiate that action

The release build may include an internal AndroidX signature permission used for app-internal components. This permission is not used to access your personal data and is not granted to other apps.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information described above only to:

  • Display and edit collages you create
  • Save and restore your recent projects on your device
  • Remember your app settings
  • Export images when you choose to do so
  • Respond if you contact us by email

We do not use your information for targeted advertising or cross-app tracking.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We do not share your photos or project data with third parties for marketing or analytics.

You may choose to share an exported image using Android’s system share features. Any recipient or app you select through the share sheet is outside StillGrid and governed by that service’s policies.

We may disclose information only if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security where legally permitted.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not embed third-party analytics, advertising, or social-login SDKs that collect personal data for us. The App works offline and does not require a network connection for core editing features.

The App includes standard Android/Jetpack libraries (for example, UI, database, and preferences components). These libraries operate within the App on your device and do not send your collage content to us.

7. Device Backup and Transfer

Android backup and device-to-device transfer may be enabled in the App configuration. If you use Google’s backup or device transfer features on your device, certain local app data (such as preferences and saved project metadata) may be copied by Android to your Google account backup or to another device according to your device settings.

We do not control Google’s backup systems. Please review your Android backup settings if you do not want this data included in cloud backup.

8. Data Retention

  • Local project data: kept on your device until you delete a project, clear app data, or uninstall the App
  • Thumbnail cache: kept until you clear it in Settings or remove app data
  • Exported images: remain in your gallery until you delete them
  • Support emails: if you email us, we retain correspondence only as long as needed to respond and maintain support records

9. Your Choices and Rights

You can:

  • Choose which photos the App can access by selecting them in the system picker
  • Delete recent projects inside the App
  • Clear thumbnail cache in Settings
  • Remove exported images from your gallery at any time
  • Delete all local App data through Android system settings or by uninstalling the App
  • Manage Android backup settings on your device

Depending on where you live, you may have additional privacy rights under applicable law. Because we do not maintain user accounts or collect personal data on our servers, many requests can be fulfilled directly by you through the actions above. You may contact us for questions about this policy.

10. Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information to us by email, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete that correspondence.

11. Security

We design the App to process data locally on your device. No method of storage or transmission is completely secure, but we do not operate backend servers that store your collage photos.

12. International Users

The App is provided for local use on your device. If you contact us by email, your message may be processed in the location where we handle support correspondence.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. The current version is available in the App under About & Legal and at the hosted policy URL published on Google Play, if provided.
